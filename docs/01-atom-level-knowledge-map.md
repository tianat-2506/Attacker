# Atom-Level Knowledge Map

Muc tieu cua file nay la chia nho kien thuc thanh cac "phan tu" co the dung de thiet ke demo, tra loi ban giam khao va huong dan team build. Moi mang co dinh nghia, lien quan toi VietSupply Radar, du lieu/chi so can co, rui ro hieu sai, vi du demo, cau hoi phan bien va nguon tham khao.

## 1. Research plan

| Supply chain/procurement | Definition, procurement flow, logistics, supplier qualification | CSCMP, Microsoft Learn, ASQ, ISO |
| Finance/fintech | Financial statements, working capital, SME finance, invoice financing | SEC, World Bank, IFC, ICC/Global SCF Forum |
| Graph/geospatial/data | Node/edge, graph centrality, GeoJSON, PostGIS, database, ETL | NetworkX, RFC 7946, PostgreSQL, PostGIS, IBM |
| AI/risk/matching | Model risk, scoring metrics, explainability, validation | NIST AI RMF, Fed SR 11-7, scikit-learn, SHAP paper |

| Blockchain | Hash, ledger, permissioned blockchain, verifiable credential | NIST FIPS 180-4, Hyperledger Fabric, W3C VC |

| 1. Supply Chain Fundamentals | Supply chain la mang cac ben tham gia sourcing, procurement, conversion, logistics va phoi hop voi supplier, intermediary, 3PL, customer. ## 3. Kien thuc toi thieu can dung trong demo

- Supply chain: node type, edge direction, lead time, MOQ, capacity, dependency, stockout, disruption.
- Procurement: RFQ, PO, goods receipt, invoice matching, approved vendor list.
- Logistics: lat/lng, province, route proxy, lead time, delivery delay, service radius.
- Quality: SKU, specification, certification, defect/return rate, substitution level.
- Finance: cash flow, runway, AR/AP, debt ratio, inventory value, late payment.
- Graph: downstream traversal, degree/out-degree, centrality, adjacency list.
- Geospatial: marker, polyline, GeoJSON, clustering, bbox.
- Algorithm: risk signal, match score, threshold, explanation, precision/recall/backtesting.
- Governance: consent, masking, RBAC, audit log, retention, secret management.

## 4. Cau hoi phan bien kho va tra loi ngan

| Cau hoi | Tra loi de pitch |
| --- | --- |
| Du lieu ban dau lay tu dau? | MVP dung synthetic data co logic; pilot lay tu anchor distributor, POS/accounting/e-invoice va survey supplier qualification. |
| AI co that khong? | MVP khong overclaim ML. He thong dung rule-based scoring minh bach va AI explanation; khi co data outcome se backtest va train ML risk model. |
| Blockchain co lam du lieu dung khong? | Khong. Blockchain chi giup du lieu da xac thuc kho bi sua va canh bao double financing; dau vao van can KYC/KYB va xac nhan da ben. |
| Co lo bi mat nha cung cap khong? | Khong cong khai graph day du. He thong dung masked nodes, aggregated view, consent va RBAC; chi mo thong tin lien he khi hai ben dong y. |
| Tai sao khong build marketplace day du? | Vi demo can chung minh luong cot loi: risk -> impact -> replacement. Marketplace day du la production roadmap. |
